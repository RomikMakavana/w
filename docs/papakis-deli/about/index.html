About Us

Welcome to Papaki's Deli: Your Gateway to Greece

A Passion for Authentic Greek Flavors

At Papaki's Deli, we're more than just a restaurant; we're a family dedicated to sharing our love for authentic Greek cuisine. We believe in using the freshest, high-quality ingredients, many of them directly imported from Greece, to create dishes that burst with flavor and transport you to the heart of the Aegean.

Our philosophy is rooted in tradition. We follow time-tested recipes passed down through generations, ensuring the authenticity of every bite. But we also understand the importance of hospitality. We want Papaki's Deli to be a warm and welcoming space where you can relax, savor delicious food, and connect with loved ones over a shared meal.

Whether you're a seasoned Greek food enthusiast or just discovering its wonders, we invite you to experience the Papaki's Deli difference. We're committed to providing you with an exceptional dining experience that captures the true essence of Greece.

Let Papaki's Deli Cater Your Next Event!

Papaki's Deli offers a wide variety of artisan Greek products, fine foods, sweet treats and a selection of beverages. Eat in or take a taste of Greece home with you. If you enjoy cooking, why not try our authentic Greek ingredients and sample your own true Greek dish.
